---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

### Current Situation
- Companies have **no visibility** into supplier emissions
- ESG compliance costs **$100K+ annually** to audit manually
- **Carbon footprint data is fragmented** across suppliers
- Companies can't identify their **biggest polluters**

### Real Impact
- Nike needs to reduce emissions by 50% by 2030
- Apple committed to carbon neutral by 2030
- Tesla must track Scope 3 emissions (suppliers)
- **No simple tool exists** to do this at scale

---

## 💡 Our Solution: CarbonChain

### What We Built
A mobile app that:
1. **Aggregates** supplier carbon data in one place
2. **Visualizes** which suppliers pollute the most
3. **Sets** reduction targets automatically
4. **Recommends** optimization actions
5. **Tracks** progress over time

### Key Features
- ✅ Add unlimited suppliers in seconds
- ✅ Real-time carbon footprint calculations
- ✅ Automated top polluter detection
- ✅ 20% reduction target recommendations
- ✅ Visual dashboards with emission rankings
- ✅ Works on any device (mobile, web, tablet)

```

    ├─ Frontend: Beautiful green UI
    ├─ Business Logic: Real-time calculations
    └─ Backend: Neo4j graph database (phase 2)
```
